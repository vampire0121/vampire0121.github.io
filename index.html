About Us

We are a premier engine component reconditioning center in Hyderabad, offering precision honing, boring, grinding, and machining with state-of-the-art equipment. Trusted by industries for performance and accuracy.

Our Machinery

Cylinder Honing Machine

This machine improves oil retention and ring seating through cross-hatch honing. It’s crucial for performance rebuilding.

Cylinder Boring Machine

It enlarges worn cylinders to factory specifications. Ensures accurate piston fit and engine performance.

Lathe Machine

For turning, facing, and threading components. It shapes precision round parts from metals.

Hydraulic Press

Provides uniform force to remove/install mechanical components. Critical for pressing and assembly operations.

Bench Drill Machine

Drills accurate holes in metal parts. Adjustable height, speed, and precision.

Crankshaft Grinding Machine

Restores crank journals to specs. Ensures smooth rotation and reduces engine wear.
